About the Issue

The urgency of wildfires demands our aid due to the devastating impact they have on the environment, communities, and economies. They destroy habitats, endanger lives, and have far-reaching economic consequences. By supporting wildfire prevention, management, and recovery efforts, we can protect lives, preserve ecosystems, and support local economies. Investing in research, technology, and community preparedness is crucial for effective prevention and response. Addressing the root causes, such as climate change, is also essential. By recognizing the urgency and offering our assistance, we can create a safer and more resilient future.

The NSWA's Solution:

  • Training
    • The National Wildfire Suppression Association (NWSA) instructor certification programs are designed to provide resources that meet or exceed all training, safety, experience, and physical fitness standards.
  • Employment
    • Our member companies are professional private firefighting companies that provide resource and firefighters to agencies on a national level. We have a job openings page for those that have posted with us. Keep checking back as we will keep posting new opening as they let us know.
