Make a Donation

The money donated here will be given to the Canadian Red Cross in an effort to support their efforts reducing wildfires around Canada. Each dollar donated will be sent towards families displaced by the fires. Other donations will also be made to organizations like Donate a Mask, an organization that provides people masks for protection against the hazardous smoke, and the Odawa Native Friendship Center, a non-profit organization that aims to help the diplaced indigenous community.
